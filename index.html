Who We Are

RightPath Development Consultancy brings together experts in human-rights due diligence, sustainable agriculture, and international certification. We specialize in practical, on-the-ground support that helps communities thrive while meeting global market expectations.

Our Approach

We center rights and people: assessing risks of forced labour and child labour, mapping deforestation risks, and designing systems that are feasible for smallholders and exporters alike.

Rights-first

Human dignity is non-negotiable in every supply chain we touch.

Forest-smart

Our Services

Certification Support (Organic & RA)

Gap analysis, ICS design, training, audit preparation, and documentation for Organic and Rainforest Alliance certifications.

EUDR Compliance (Deforestation & Forced Labour)

Full due diligence systems: geolocation, supply chain mapping, deforestation risk assessment, and human-rights risk mitigation.

Human-Rights Due Diligence

Forced labour risk assessments, remediation pathways, grievance mechanisms, and worker-centered monitoring systems.

Capacity Building & Training

Training-of-trainers, farmer field schools, cooperative governance, and practical guides on rights and sustainability.

Monitoring, Evaluation & Research

Impact measurement, baseline studies, and participatory monitoring to ensure continuous improvement and accountability.
